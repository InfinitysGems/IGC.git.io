About Infinity Stones

Infinity Stones is a Defi ecosystem composed of two Defi Tokens and three Farming contracts, hosted on the Tron Blockchain, transparent and opensource. It is the first Defi Dapp that is easy to use, thanks to the "One Click Staking" function, and safe thanks to it's embedded secured-liquidity mechanism. All liquidity is always locked forever, by default. This offers a great protection against impermanent losses also known as "rug pull", since nobody can take advantage of the system. Infinity Stones and Infinity Gems price is totally dictated by supply and demand dynamics, the whole dapp is fully decentralized all the time. Infinity Stones can be bought and sold at any time, and they can be also used to farm Infinity Gems! Infinity Gems can be bought and sold at any time! Infinity Stones is an entertainment vehicle and not an investment product.
